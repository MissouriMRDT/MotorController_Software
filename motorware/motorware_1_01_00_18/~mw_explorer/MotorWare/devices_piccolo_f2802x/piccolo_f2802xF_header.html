Piccolo F2802xF


Key features

  • Up to 60-MHz, 32-bit Fixed-Point C28x™ CPU
  • 64-kB or 32-kB embedded Flash, 10kB RAM
  • 12-bit ADC, up to 3.46 MSPS
  • 4ePWM modules with 8 total ePWM outputs
  • 4 HRPWM channels
  • 1 SPI, 1 UART, 1 I2C
  • 48-pin LQFP, -40 to 105 or -40 to 125C AEC Q100
  • Motor ROM including FAST™ software encoder
    and InstaSPIN™-FOC solutions


Key benefits

  • Leading 32-bit performance for real-time control
  • Unique, precision control peripherals
  • Lowers system cost
  • Increased ease of use
  • Small package size
